YOUR FAVORITES

Caramel  Macchiato

Latte macchiato is a coffee beverage; the name means stained or marked milk. Marked as in an espresso stain on the milk used. It is a play on "Espresso Macchiato" which is an espresso with a drop or two of milk or cream.

165 - 185 Php

Grande Froccino Primo Chocolate Chips

My friend opted for their Froccino. Yep, this would seem like a total rip-off of Starbucks' Frappuccino based on the name but tastewise, no comparison there. She chose their Chocolate chip Froccino Primo and according to her, it was good. I just find it odd that they placed a sleeve in the cup, yet, it's a cold drink.

145 Php

 Cafe Caramel Latte

If you're looking for a sweeter latte, Caramel Latte is for you. I didn't need to add sugar as the caramel is enough to make the latte sweet enough. However, my friend thought of the Chocolate Chips 'froccino primo a bit bitter.

125 php

Hazel Nut Chocolate Latte

It is a combination of impeccably smooth cream, the richness of chocolate, and of course, the rich flavor of roasted hazelnuts. You can have it as a hot latte (left side of the photo) when you're in need of a hot embrace, or as an ice blended (right side) when you'd rather chill. 

145 - 195 Php

Java Chip Frappuccino

What's in it: Coffee syrup is  blended with milk and ice to form the base, then combined with mocha sauce and Frappuccino chips and finally, topped with whipped cream and mocha syrup.

150 - 180 Php

Original Mocha

The frozen sensation that started it all contains our delicious coffee extract and our special dutch, chocolate powder and skimmed milk, blended with ice then topped with whipped cream.
