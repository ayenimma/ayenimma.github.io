INTERVIEW

We got some thoughts from people we met inside the coffee shop. I guess, they do love drinking coffee.

Interviewee 1

Student

As we talk to her, we really enjoyed since we are all students, we could actually relate to each other. According to her, she often go straight to Starbucks after school to grab her favorite Caramel Macchiato.

Interviewee 2

Staff

It's fascinating to know that training for Starbucks actually takes a long period of time. Well, atleast you get to smell coffee each day of your life, the long wait is totally worth it. She said that she likes going to work since she is also very fond of drinking coffee so Starbucks does feel like home for her.

Interviewee 3

Group of Friends

As much as our friends love to spend time inside a coffee shop, they do too! And it proves that coffee shops never really fails to give people memories and moments that they will probably treasure forever. They said that spilling teas will never be complete without a sip of coffee.
